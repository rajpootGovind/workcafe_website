JOBAAJ Work Café is where focused work meets thoughtful design — created inside the JOBAAJ Group and opened for those who work with intent.

How It Started

Before it was a café, JOBAAJ Work Café was an internal workspace.

As part of the JOBAAJ Group, our teams spend long hours thinking, building, and solving problems. We needed a space that respected focus, reduced noise, and made work feel sustainable.

So we built one.

Over time, visitors, partners, and collaborators experienced the space — and asked the same question: “Can we work here too?”

That’s when JOBAAJ Work Café opened its doors beyond our own teams — keeping the same standards, the same calm, and the same intention.

Part of the JOBAAJ Group

JOBAAJ Work Café is an initiative of the JOBAAJ Group — a company built by people who work deeply, think long-term, and value quality over speed.

Because the space was designed for our own employees first, every detail has been tested in real working conditions — not imagined for marketing.

The result is a work café that feels natural to work in, day after day.
